Last updated: June 2025

This Cookies Policy explains how PropinDubai (“we”, “us”, or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function efficiently and provide analytical information.

Why do we use cookies?

  • Essential cookies – for core website functionality (such as session management).
  • Performance cookies – to understand website usage and improve user experience.
  • Advertising cookies – to deliver relevant ads and track the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.
  • Third-party cookies – provided by analytics and advertising partners (such as Google Analytics).

Your choices

You can control or delete cookies via your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to use some features of our website.
