Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

PropinDubai (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you interact with our platform, including our website.

By accessing or using PropinDubai’s services, you agree to the terms of this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal data: Name, phone number, email, location, nationality, budget, and any information you submit via forms, messages or calls.
  • Browsing data: IP address, device info, browser type, access times, pages viewed.
  • Financial and mortgage-related data: If you inquire about financial or mortgage services, we may collect data such as salary, credit score, or bank info via third-party partners.
  • Interaction data: Information gathered from your communications with agents or developers via the platform.

3. How We Collect Your Data

  • When you register or submit a form on our website
  • When you communicate with agents or our team
  • From partners such as developers, mortgage advisors, or advertising providers
  • Automatically via cookies and analytics tools when browsing

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To connect you with real estate agents or developers
  • To improve our platform and customize your experience
  • To send alerts, updates, or marketing offers if you opt in
  • To analyse site traffic and improve services
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect our legal interests

5. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Your consent when applicable
  • Contractual necessity (e.g. when you request a service)
  • Legal obligations
  • Our legitimate business interests

6. Who We Share Your Data With

  • Real estate agents or developers you choose to contact
  • Service providers such as email, hosting, and analytics partners
  • Mortgage or financial institutions (if relevant)
  • Legal authorities when required

7. International Data Transfers

We may store or process your data outside the UAE. In such cases, we will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with data protection regulations.

8. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system can be completely secure. You are responsible for keeping any login credentials confidential.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by law. We evaluate the amount and type of data retained to ensure minimization.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request data portability

To exercise your rights, contact us at info@propindubai.com.
